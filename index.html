against empire

tom wilson, 2025
@ egress media — naarm

Against Empire is a studio journal and theoretical manuscript by tom wilson and others. The first edition (October 2025) was published by egress media (Naarm/Melbourne) and developed within the context of the RMIT BFA, Year 1—while standing as an independent work.

The book collects a semester-length studio journal alongside essays, dialogues, and documentation of works-in-progress and resolved pieces. It organises the material across six chapters: Burden, Asymptotia, Virtue, Action, Violence, and Essays.

The project traces practice-led research in conversation with critical theory, linking studio experiments (e.g. Thermal Poem Stack, Asymptotic Copy, agar/wax studies) to conceptual frames. It engages anti-imperialism, colonialism, decolonisation, and institutional critique—inviting readers to examine how power is aestheticised and to consider practical alternatives in art and everyday life.
