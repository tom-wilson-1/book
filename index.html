Against Empire is a studio journal and theoretical manuscript by Tom Wilson in conversation with many. The first edition (October 2025) documents his creative research and writing for the Bachelor of Fine Arts (RMIT) and explores themes of anti‑imperialism, colonialism, decolonisation, and creative practice. This work is released into the commons and may be freely reproduced, distributed and adapted without attribution or restriction.
